Strategy

Organic-first, paid as an optional accelerator

Philosophy

Maximize free distribution and local relevance before spending. Paid only amplifies what organic already proves works.

  • Owned: Promo page is the hub — every channel links here or to the official listing
  • Earned: Local groups, agent network, lender co-marketing
  • Creative reuse: One photo set → five channel-native formats
  • Optional paid: Geo-targeted Meta + Search after UTMs and baseline traffic exist
Organic Channels

Where we distribute — zero ad spend

Listing portals & marketplaces

  • Zillow, Realtor.com, Redfin syndication (via MLS/broker feed where available)
  • Facebook Marketplace — home + land listing with story creative
  • LandWatch / Lands of America — acreage angle
  • Craigslist Dallas / Waco / Central TX — refreshed weekly

Local & hyperlocal

  • Facebook groups: Mexia, Limestone County, Waco homebuyers, first-time buyer TX
  • Nextdoor — Mexia + surrounding ZIPs
  • Yard sign with QR → promo page URL

Week 1 priority

Portal syndication live, Marketplace post up, yard sign ordered, UTM links generated for every channel.

Content & Social

Short-form video and evergreen posts

  • Instagram / Facebook / TikTok: 9:16 story creative + drone walkthrough clips (deck, acreage, interior)
  • YouTube Shorts: "New construction under $150K on nearly an acre" hook
  • Pinterest: 2:3 pin creative → landing page (rural home, small house, Texas land)
  • Google Business Profile: Elegment Land + property post with link
  • Email / SMS: Blast to existing Elegment buyer list + warm agent contacts

Cadence: 3–4 posts/week Weeks 1–4, then 2/week maintenance. Rotate exterior → land → financing → lifestyle angles.

Partnerships

Agents, lenders, and referrals

Buyer's agents (co-broke)

One-pager + creative kit to Waco, Dallas, and Limestone County agents. Emphasize turnkey, financing, and low taxes.

Lender / financing partners

FHA, VA, USDA-friendly LOs co-post "payment from ~$1,150/mo" content (with required disclaimers). Pre-qual referral path.

Referral incentive

Structured referral fee or gift card for buyer referrals that close — terms per Elegment policy. Track via unique UTM/referral codes.

Employer / relocation

  • Target employers with Waco/Dallas commuter bases
  • Highlight affordability vs. metro rent
  • Offer virtual tour + weekend showing blocks
Optional Paid Layer

Small budget test — only after organic baseline

Suggested range (options, not commitments): $500–$1,500/month for 4–8 weeks.

  • Meta: Geo-radius Waco + Dallas + Limestone County — traffic or lead ads using sq/portrait creatives; retarget site visitors
  • Google Search: "homes for sale Mexia TX," "Limestone County land home," "FHA home Texas" — exact + phrase match
  • Featured boosts: Zillow/Realtor promoted placement if available
  • Hold reserve (10%): Pause or reallocate if CPA exceeds target

Gate: organic UTMs live 2+ weeks, landing page CTR baseline established, CEO sign-off on monthly cap.
